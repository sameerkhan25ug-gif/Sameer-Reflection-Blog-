Studying unit 4 was not just about theoretical learning but about learning by self and through hands-on practical projects. Which made this unit both- simultaneously enjoyable and challenging.

The reason behind choosing Information Science as a major at APU was, it is a course which promotes projects based learning and that I truly experienced during this unit. Here I encountered 2 projects: **Chatbot and Metabase**. To be honest both the topics were equally strange to me as I had no clue about it. Moreover there were no instructions and sources provided from the instructor and we were supposed to finish it in 7 days, which made it a bit challenging.

However as soon as I started working on it, I found it was not so hard. I was able to find various online resources such as **tutorials, articles** and lastly **learning from real chatbot about their core aspects and function** helped me to understand the concept of the  projects in depth. Moreover, having a well **structured plan** about gathering information, processing it and  executing it helped to overcome time constraints and finish on time.

The only place I was stuck was in the metabase. Where I was unable to apply multiple filters on a single data. However constant practicing and learning helped to overcome it. This was the first time I challenged myself and built some qualities that I was lacking with. I am the person who is so addicted to spoon feeding but this time, I was able to execute the whole process from gathering the resources, understanding concepts and executing them. This helped me to build a quality of **resourcefulness** and **an optimistic attitude**.

Moreover at the end of the project I was able to understand whole mechanism about the real chatbots that we use in daily life- like **Disha 2.0** for railway queries and **Amazon chatbot**  for product queries. Moreover, I learned how we can analyze a huge set of data and represent it in a **concise** and **effective** way through a metabase. And  I personally feel learning metabase will truly help me as an Information Science student as I will deal with huge data so I will have to analyze and represent in a concise and comprehensible way.
