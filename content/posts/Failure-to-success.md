In this unit we learned about the **‘FAILURE’**. This is something that we all are familiar with, as we are dealing with it since childhood. However, this time experience was new towards failure. As this unit stood thought provoking which helped me learn how to turn **stepback** into **stepping stones**.

While dealing with this unit I found I had enough knowledge about failure. But on other hand I was lacking with how to classify failure? Are all failures same ? How to react to different failure ? etc. And all these questions got clear during this unit.

I could relate this unit most to my personal life. As being an Information Science student I do encounter failure during coding. And most often the question that arises in my mind is that am I less capable of understanding this concept? Why I encounter error/bugs again and again, as it reflects failure. However, later I realised that I have spirit that I don’t give up easily and I try to overcome failure. This made me realised that each time when I failed in coding it reflects that I am trying and I am giving my sincere attempt. As a result I got strong belief on statement that _'**failing is first step in learning'.**_


The biggest problem that I faced was working in a group. I found that people don’t contribute equally, which I find as an injustice with one who gives more effort. However, at same time I found the key for this issue. I learned that how through **effective communication** we can build a team and by allocating an equal and workable task to a respective person we can bring effective result. To bring this alive one should take charge for others. And I find , I did that work quite well in my group. As we ended up with great success in project.
