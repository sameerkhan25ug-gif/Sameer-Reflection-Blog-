I think that I knew how to convince people but after studying DIKW , I got to know about a more formal way for the approach. At same time I got to know how wiki is made and enhance using the markdown.

All this was a bit hard for me. However, I find working in a group makes  work precise and easily understandable. Lastly the only way I found to overcome what I was lacking was through ‘PRACTICE’ as its most convenient method to get savvy with data .
